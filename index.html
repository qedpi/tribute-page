To My Parents

For the gift of Life and Love

Let me count my thanks:


Born into humble backgrounds, they studied hard, aspiring for a better life

They relocated twice, to different countries, in order to give me a brighter future

They had to learn two new Languages, struggled to find employment, while taking care of me

They endured long times apart, in order to keep the family together

They are my greatest supporters, while being approachable like close friends

They accept my flaws, support my attempts at betterment, and believe in me

I want them to know that I have not forgotten their sacrifices

In time, and in turn, and in tune with filial piety

I will return this gift of a better life to them

Thank you, for the gift of life, for the gift of love!

Composed & Coded by Thomas J. Hu
